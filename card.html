Cat

A group of cats is called a “clowder.”

  • Scientific Name: Felis catus
  • LifeSpan: 15 Years
  • Gestation period: 64-67 Days
  • Daily Sleep: 12-16 Hrs

Cats, also called domestic cats (Felis catus), are carnivorous (meat-eating) mammals, of the family Felidae. Cats have been domesticated (tame) for nearly 10,000 years.They are currently the most popular pets in the world. Their origin is probably the African Wildcat Felis silvestris lybica.
